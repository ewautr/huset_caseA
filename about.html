Bastard Café – board games, coffee and hygge!

WHO are we?

On the ground floor of Huset, you will find the popular board game café Bastard Café. Here you can play all kinds of board games and enjoy a nice creamy cup of coffee, a great micro brewed beer or get some goods for your tummy with the delicious fries and toast the café offers.

Bastard Café has expanded with the basement. Here you can also enjoy all types of board games but you can also experience a bit more of a bar atmosphere and try out some of their amazing gin!

It dosen’t matter if you are an expert in games or only tried ludo. Here there is room for everbody. Bastard Café also have volunteer gaming gurus to help you with understanding the rules of their many gaming opportunities.

You can also joins them for one of their fun theme nights. Bastard Café throws tournaments og social meetups where you can get to know new people and games.
